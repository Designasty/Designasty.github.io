About Nonna's Trattoria

About us

Nonna's Trattoria originated in 2022. Our name is based off of the Italian translation for “Grandma's Restaurant,” or as we know it, Nonna's Trattoria! Italian cuisine is enjoyed by millions, just like we enjoyed it throughout our lives in the heart of Italy. But, why are we here in New Hope? After the passing of our late Nonna, we wanted to bring authentic cuisine and a casual dining experience to YOU. Just the way Nonna did for us!

Our Mission

Nonna's Trattoria's mission is to provide you with the opportunity to build relationships and memories in the company of traditional Italian cuisine, the way Nonna used to.
